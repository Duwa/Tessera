HAV Formula
HAV(h, session) = 0.50 × NPF + 0.30 × SRQ + 0.20 × OC
NPF — Novel Problem Frequency · how often the person encounters situations requiring genuine judgment (weight: 50%)
SRQ — Stakeholder Recovery Quality · quality of human recovery when AI fails (weight: 30%)
OC — Organisational Coherence · consistency of decisions across the org's value structure (weight: 20%)

How onboarding data import works
1. Map your data → HAV components
NPF = non-routine work fraction (from Workday task codes or survey Q1-Q2). SRQ = escalation recovery quality (from ticket system or Q3). OC = novel framing events (managerial assessment or Q4-Q5). Overall score distributes as 50/30/20.
2. One period = one T&A session
Each quarter, month, or performance cycle becomes a completed T&A session. Sessions are time-stamped to when the period ended. Tessera orders them chronologically and computes HAV trend (improving / stable / declining) from the slope.
3. Aggregate-HAV auto-runs
After import, aggregate-hav immediately writes "Live HAV (T&A Measured)" performance reviews for every employee with data. The twin then has real φ-calibration input. You go from zero to a calibrated org in one POST.
